Title: Recycle.kg
Slogan: "Sort with Us"
Recycle.kg is a volunteer organization with a strong focus on environmental conservation. Currently comprised of 89 dedicated volunteers, our mission is encapsulated in the slogan, "Sort with Us." As a co-founder of Recycle.kg, I have actively contributed to the cause by conducting over 4 ecology lessons for elementary school students. Additionally, we have installed plastic recycling bins in four schools and two branches of English language courses.

Our unique approach involves collecting plastic waste from these bins, sending it for recycling, and using the proceeds to fund tree planting initiatives. By converting plastic waste into financial resources, we not only contribute to waste reduction but also actively participate in reforestation efforts.

In addition to these ongoing projects, Recycle.kg has organized more than 5 community cleanup events on Saturdays, engaging volunteers in the collective effort to rid our city of litter. Through these initiatives, we strive to foster a sense of environmental responsibility and instill the importance of waste separation and recycling in our community.
Join us in making a positive impact on the environment. "Sort with Us" at Recycle.kg and be a part of the movement towards a cleaner, greener future.
