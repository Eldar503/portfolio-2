Title: ITcaBIS
Slogan: "Education for All"

ITcaBIS is a non-profit organization founded with the mission of providing education opportunities for underprivileged families. As a co-founder of ITcaBIS, my commitment is to bridge the educational gap and empower those in need. Our slogan, "Education for All," encapsulates our dedication to making learning accessible to everyone.

Focusing on Front End development, ITcaBIS currently operates with two groups, catering to individuals from economically disadvantaged backgrounds. Additionally, we have a third group specifically tailored for senior citizens, where we assist them in learning to use smartphones. Our teaching sessions for seniors take place in retirement homes, creating a supportive and interactive learning environment.

Collaborating with schools, we have established two offices in the city that serve as hubs for our educational initiatives. These partnerships with schools enhance our reach and allow us to identify and assist those who would benefit the most from our programs.

In response to the evolving educational landscape, ITcaBIS is proactive in adapting to the digital age. We have launched an online learning platform and plan to expand our reach nationally this year. Looking ahead, our ambitious goals include establishing a presence in Kazakhstan and Israel within the next year, further extending the impact of our educational initiatives.
